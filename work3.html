Biography

experimental psychedelic rock band, born in a remote community of Venezuela with the name of Ciudad Guayana, is the union of three friends trying to make good music, NATANIEL ROSE on guitar and ANDERSON CALDERON on drums were the first members along with their vocalist ABRAHAW MALAV3, after a change in the drums when GONZALO MURUA enters, the first album called CONTIGO is made around the year 2010 produced and recorded by NESTOR MOYA in the city of Puerto Ordaz creating music much faster than the usual punk, when listening to the album you can hear a very accelerated drum intentionally, then in 2013 the album EFECTO PARA RESUCITAR which is produced by jefrey batista, bassist who enters to provide a heavy sound and changes closer to acid rock, album produced and recorded at the La Barraca theater in the city of San Felix, its principles the band was clearly punk, over time they were obtaining a more Californian wave approaching fastcore or skatecore with fast and strident guitars and an accelerated drum, time passes and obtains this experimental and fresh sound with harmonic touches, pleasant to listen to, often postponing the climaxes of acid rock, currently its lineup is HUGO LOPEZ on drums with rhythm and strong sound makes the band sound in a beastly way, CESAR SALAZAR lead guitarist creating a unique and genuine sound, ABRAHAW MALAV3 guitar and vocals, main founder of the band.​
